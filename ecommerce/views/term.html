Terms

1.B2C (Business to Consumer): The most common form, where businesses sell directly to consumers (e.g., Amazon, Shopify stores). 2.B2B (Business to Business): Companies sell products or services to other businesses (e.g., Alibaba). 3.C2C (Consumer to Consumer): Individuals sell directly to other consumers, often through platforms like eBay, Craigslist, or Poshmark. 4.C2B (Consumer to Business): Consumers sell their products or services to businesses (e.g., freelance platforms like Upwork).
